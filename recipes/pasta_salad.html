Pasta Salad

A quick recipe perfect for meal prepping

  • One pound of tri-colored pasta
  • Italian style dressing
  • 6 Tbsp salad seasoning
  • 2 cups of diced cherry tomatoes
  • One green pepper, chopped
  • One red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • A can of black olives, chopped
  1. Bring water to a boil and then cook the pasta for 10-12 minutes and drain it
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
  4. Refrigerate salad for at least 8 hours.
