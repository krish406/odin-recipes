Quesadilla

A Mexican dish perfect for a quick and easy dinner

This recipe is made by placing refried beans, cheese, sour cream and salsa inside of a tortilla.

  • Tortillas
  • Fiesta Blend Shreeded Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Green Pepper
  • Onion
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Butter
  1. Turn the stove onto medium heat
  2. Place a pan on the stove and place butter on the pan
  3. Dice your onion and green pepper and place them on the pan
  4. Once these veggies have sauted, put the refried beans on the pan
  5. Season the beans with taco seasoning
  6. Once this has cooked, use another pan to cook the quesidillas. Turn the stove on medium heat and place butter on the pan
  7. Place the tortilla on the butter
  8. While the bottom cooks, spread a little butter across the top
  9. Flip the tortilla
  10. Once both sides are slightly browned, spread beans and cheese on one side and close the tortilla
  11. Cook the quesidilla until the cheese is fully melted. Place sour cream and salsa inside the quesidilla once it has been cooked
